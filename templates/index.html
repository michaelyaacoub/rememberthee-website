Remember Thee

Improve your recall of Scripture

How it works

1

Add verses you want to remember. Make a note of what God is saying to you.

2

Review and be encouraged by what God has done. Make flashcards for memorization.

3

Organize verses by category. Sort by order in the Bible or when you most recently added them.

Search Quickly

It’s easy to find verses you know you’ve inputted into Remember Thee. Avoid sifting through old notes, journals, the Bible or Google search results.

Share Confidently

Remember Thee is your own personal library of Scripture you love. Have on hand to share verses you know and love that God has given you.

Correlate Powerfully

Categories help correlate verses you love. Correlation improves retention and will help you more effectively interpret Scripture with Scripture.
